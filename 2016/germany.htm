PUPC International Chapter in Germany

The PUPC has established an international chapter at the Freshman Institute of FH Aachen - Aachen University of Applied Sciences in Geilenkirchen, Germany. If you are a prospective contestant who could not travel to the U.S. for the Onsite Test at Princeton, you now have the opportunity to compete at the Freshman Institute of FH Aachen instead.

Results: Onsite Test at Freshman Institute of FH Aachen

22 students competed in the first annual Onsite Test of the Princeton University Physics Competition based at the Freshman Institute of FH Aachen, held on Saturday, November 21, 2015. The rankings are now available here.
For your individual score breakdown, please e-mail us at psps@princeton.edu or Soheil Mirzapoor (soheil.mirzapoor *AT* alumni.fh-aachen.de).

You may access the problem packets that you were given here.

We congratulate all participants, whether you are a winner or not!
Thank you very much and we hope to see you, along with your classmates and friends, next year.
