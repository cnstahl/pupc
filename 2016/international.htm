International Chapters

The PUPC has established three international chapters in addition to its headquarters at Princeton University.

PUPC 2015 featured one chapter at the South University of Science and Technology of China (SUSTC) in Shenzhen, China and one chapter at the Freshman Institute of FH Aachen in Geilenkirchen, Germany.

PUPC 2016 will include an additional chapter at Comenius University (Faculty of Mathematics, Physics and Informatics) in Bratislava, Slovakia.

If you are a prospective contestant who could not travel to the U.S. for the Onsite Test at Princeton, you now have the opportunity to compete at one of these locations instead.
