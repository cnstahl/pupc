Privacy Policy

What We Collect: Users of the PSPS Portal must register an account with a valid princeton.edu email address. Administrators of the site can see the comments, email addresses, and IP addresses of the site's users. The questions in the Physics Connect section are completely anonymous; neither users nor moderators/administrators can see the user or IP address associated with any particular question.

What We Do and Don't Do With Your Information: We will not disclose any of our users' personally identifying information, such as full IP address, e-mail address, or Princeton netID. We will not share our users' personal information with other sites, third parties, or even other users of the site. We do, however, reserve the discretionary right to disclose the netID or e-mail address to the proper authorities if a user threatens harm to him- or herself or to another user.

Changes to the Privacy Policy: We reserve the right to make changes to our privacy policy at any time. Users should check this page for the latest privacy policy.

 

Terms of Use

Users of the PSPS Portal agree to refrain from posting links to spam, malware, or pornography. Explicit or obscene content will be removed at the moderators' discretion. Threats, flaming, and inappropriate content are not permitted. Violators will be given a warning or a ban from the site, depending on the severity of the offense(s). Please remember, this site is stationed on servers that belong to Princeton University, and as such all University regulations apply.
