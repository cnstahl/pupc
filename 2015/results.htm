Results: Onsite Test

Approximately 120 students competed in our second annual Onsite Test of the Princeton University Physics Competition, held on Saturday, November 21, 2015. For the first time, the Onsite Test was synchronously held outside of Princeton University, in two other locations around the globe: South University of Science and Technology of China (SUSTC) in Shenzhen, China and the Freshman Institute of FH Aachen - Aachen University of Applied Sciences in Geilenkirchen, Germany.

The rankings for each of the three chapters are accessible from the following pages:

  • Princeton University (USA)

  • South University of Science and Technology of China

  • Freshman Institute of FH Aachen (Germany)

Thank you in advance for your time!
